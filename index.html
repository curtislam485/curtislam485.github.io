About

Background & Skills

I'm an electrical engineer with a focus on analog and mixed-signal design. I got into EE because I wanted to understand how physical systems actually behave — not just simulate them, but build them and see where theory meets reality.

My design process starts with the physics. Before touching a schematic I want to understand the fundamental constraints: noise floors, thermal limits, bandwidth-power tradeoffs. The circuit follows from that, not the other way around.

I'm currently studying at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and looking for internship and research opportunities in analog or power electronics.
